Las Cotorras

Technical details

Developed in Unity

Single-player

Genre: Survival

Platform: Computer

Summary

In this video game you take the role of a person who has a very annoying family. You are locked in your house with visitors and your mother will not let you hide in your room, so you will have to go around the house dodging all the people who want to bother you, or you will lose all your mental health.

Mechanics

At the start of the game, the player finds themselves in a house where they will have to survive for a period of time. The time is indicated in the upper right corner. In the upper left corner is their mental health bar. If this bar reaches zero they will die. The player moves around the map with the "WASD" keys.

Enemies and NPCs

There are two types of enemies. The static enemy, which prevents you from being in certain rooms. If you enter a room with a static enemy you will start to hear their screams and your mental health will go down. The other enemy is the moving enemy. They move from one fixed point to another. When you are close to them you will hear a sound that indicates you are in their damage area.

Apart from this there is another character, your mother, who prevents you from going upstairs and hiding from visitors.
