Spaceship Game

Technical details

Developed in Unity

Single-player

Genre: Real-time action

Platform: Computer

Summary

A game in which you take control of a spaceship and have to avoid different obstacles to avoid dying.

Mechanics

The player controls the horizontal movement while the ship moves forward on its own at a constant speed. The game is paused with the "ESC" key.

The game has a point system that is calculated by the distance travelled. It also has a best score system, so you can play it several times and see if you can beat your score.

Power-ups

Blue power-ups increase your speed temporarily and green power-ups increase your points.

Dangers

There are four types of dangers:

Mines, which are the static dangers.

Asteroids that go from one point to another. They move at a constant speed from one predetermined point to another.

Rotating asteroids, which are more elongated rocks that rotate around themselves at a constant speed.

Doors, which open when you are close to them, but you have to be careful to go through the area that opens and not crash into the wall. They also make it difficult to see.
