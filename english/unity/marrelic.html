Mar's Relic

Technical details

Developed in Unity

Single-player

Genre: Adventure, 3D Platformer

Platform: Computer

Summary

In this single-player adventure game you take the role of Nox, an anthropomorphic cat who opens a mysterious box in a forest that makes him small.

A dragon named Mar scolds him for opening the box and explains that to return to his size he will have to retrieve her relic.

Nox will have to fight his way through the insects until he reaches the relic thanks to a weapon given to him by Mar, who will be his companion throughout the adventure.

Mechanics

The player can move with the "WASD" keys or the arrow keys and the space key to jump. Left click for melee attack, right click for shooting. To look around you have to move the mouse.

The game can also be played with an XBox controller. You can move with the left joystick and look around with the right joystick. You jump with the "A" key and attack with the "X" key. You can also pause the game at any time with "ESC" or with the pause button on the controller.

The menu can be navigated either with the controller or with the keyboard, not only with the mouse.

The player has a health bar that drops if an enemy attacks him. There are objects on the map that can be used to recover health. If you run out of health you die.

Dangers

The game features a real-time combat system. There are three types of enemies, which are different coloured ants with different characteristics.

The other danger is water, if the player falls into the water they will die instantly.
